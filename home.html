    Welcome to the Caribbean Cricket Podcast (CCP).

    The CCP Team

    The Caribbean Cricket Podcast was set up as a Caribbean diaspora media initiative. The aim was and remains to promote diversity in the communication of cricket news from the Caribbean.

    Machel St Patrick Hewitt
    Founder

    Machel St Patrick Hewitt is a Jamaican-born, South London-raised freelance sports journalist and broadcaster. He is most often found behind a mic or laptop in sports grounds across the country.

    Santokie Nagulendran
    Co-Founder

    Santokie Nagulendran is a Guyanese-born freelance sports journalist cricket. From a rich sporting background, he is known for insightful analysis and engaging storytelling.
